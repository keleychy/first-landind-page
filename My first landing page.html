WHO WE ARE

Techrise is an institution owned by the state government of Abia. This is to train youth from all 17 local government of the state on tech related skills which in turn will be of benefit to the state and the nation Nigeria at large.

For a start, 30 youths were selected from each local government of the state to participate in the cohort 1 of the programme. The programme will last for 3 months and another 3 months job placement
